About

In the midst of all the chaos and commotion we have almost forgotten the golden period of our childhood. It is encapsulated forever in our memories. So why not travel down the memory lane to those beautiful times. This Hill'ffair, with the theme *Horror of the Hills* we take on a journey to reminisciate some of the most ineradicable moments we have lived! Yes it's all in the past...but what if we could somehow relive it. Let's bring those innocent, vivacious and felicitous times back into our present. Let's come together to celebrate the momentousness of the horror of hills and make it another memory to last a lifetime !
